What is Concetto?

Concetto is the flagship techno-cultural fest of the Department of Civil Engineering, GEC Thrissur. Blending technical brilliance with cultural vibrancy, the fest brings together competitive technical events, hands-on workshops, engaging informals, and high-energy pro shows.

From problem-solving and innovation to music, performances, and celebration, Concetto is a three-day platform where ideas compete, talent shines, and experiences come alive.
